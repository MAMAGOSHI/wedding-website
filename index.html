Our Story

Our story began in May 2023, when what seemed like a simple connection quickly turned into something special. By August, we made it official and from then on, it was always us.

Soon after, distance became part of our journey when Maphefo moved to Manchester. It wasn’t always easy, but it brought us closer in the ways that mattered most, teaching us patience, trust, and how to truly show up for each other on an emotional and spiritual level.

In 2025, we said “yes” to forever, a moment that felt as natural as everything that led us there. No doubt, the support of our families on that day made it extra special.

Now, we’re so excited to begin this next chapter together, where we finally get to close that long-distance gap. Our story has been filled with love, laughter, and growth and we know the best is yet to come.

We can’t wait to celebrate with you 🥳
